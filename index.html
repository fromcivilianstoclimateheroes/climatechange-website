What is Climate Change all about?

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in temperature and weather patterns. While Earth's temperatures do change over time, human activities, especially the burning of fossil fuels, have made major changes.

Main Causes

Burning Fossil Fuels

Toxic gases like coal and oil release greenhouse gases that trap heat in the atmosphere.

Deforestation

Trees absorb carbon dioxide, and cutting them down will increase CO₂ levels, which is harmful to the environment.

Industrial Activities

Factories and transportation produce huge amounts of emissions, which will make the air toxic.

Effects on Our Planet

Rising Temperatures

Global temperatures, like Global Warming, are increasing, which leads to heatwaves.

Melting Ice & Sea Level Rise

Ice in some countries like Antartica, are rapidly melting, which also results to rising sea levels due to melted ice.

Extreme Weather

Natural disasters like Landslides and floods will happen more often.

What can you do?

  • Turning off lights when not in use can save energy
  • Walk or use a transportation that doesn't involve any toxic chemicals
  • do the 3R's (Reduce, Reuse, Recycle)
  • Create or join small communities that help lessen climate change

Every Action Counts.

simple actions that we can do in our daily lives can make a huge difference for our planet.
